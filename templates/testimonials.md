# Testimonials

Add testimonials from your clients or customers. These appear on your homepage and/or a dedicated testimonials page. Real testimonials with names and photos build trust.

Copy the template block for each testimonial.

---

## Testimonial

**Client Name**: {e.g., Sarah Nakamya}

**Client Role / Title**: {e.g., CEO, Nakamya Enterprises — or just "Client" if they prefer}

**Company / Organisation**: {e.g., Nakamya Enterprises — or N/A for individual clients}

**Quote**: {The testimonial in their words — e.g., "We hired them to build our family home and the experience was exceptional. They finished on time, within budget, and the quality exceeded our expectations. I recommend them to anyone looking to build in Kampala."}

**Photo**: {e.g., sarah-nakamya.jpg — or leave blank if no photo}

**Date**: {e.g., January 2025 — or leave blank}

**Service Used**: {e.g., Residential Construction — helps us link testimonials to relevant services}

**Rating**: {e.g., 5 — out of 5 stars, or leave blank}
