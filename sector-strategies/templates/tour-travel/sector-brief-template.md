## Brand Colors

Define your primary brand colors as hex codes. These override sector defaults and guide all design decisions.

**Primary Brand Color**: (e.g., #C97655 Terracotta)
*Used for: Headers, primary CTAs, brand identity*

**Secondary Color**: (e.g., #2D5A4F Forest Green)
*Used for: Accents, secondary CTAs, trust signals*

**Accent Color**: (e.g., #E8B84A Sunset Gold)
*Used for: Highlights, badges, interactive elements*

**Background Color**: (e.g., #FAF7F1 Cream/Offwhite)
*Used for: Page backgrounds, card backgrounds*

**Text Color**: (e.g., #2C2C2C Charcoal)
*Used for: Primary text, headers*

**Secondary Text**: (e.g., #8B8680 Stone Gray)
*Used for: Body text, descriptions, muted content*
