# E-commerce: Design Tokens Reference

## Color Philosophy
Conversion-focused. Bold accent for CTAs. Trust neutrals for credibility. Social proof visible.

**Sector Standard:**
- Primary: Bold accent (varies by category)
- CTA: High-contrast (red, orange, or blue)
- Background: Clean white
- Trust: Green (in-stock), Gray (secondary)

## Typography
- Product names: Bold sans-serif
- Descriptions: Readable serif or sans-serif
- CTAs: Large, semibold, high contrast

## Key Components
- **Product Cards**: Image, name, price, reviews, add-to-cart CTA
- **Add to Cart**: Prominent, always visible on product
- **Checkout CTA**: Clear, fast, trust badges (secure payment icons)
- **Reviews Section**: Star rating, customer testimonials, verified purchase badge
