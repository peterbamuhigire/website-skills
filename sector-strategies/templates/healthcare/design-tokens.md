# Healthcare: Design Tokens Reference

## Color Philosophy
Trust, accessibility, calm. Blues/greens convey safety. High contrast for readability.

**Sector Standard Palette:**
- Primary: Calming Blue (#0369A1)
- Secondary: Healthcare Green (#059669)
- Accent: Warm (#D97706)
- Backgrounds: Clean white/light gray

## Typography
- Headers: Professional sans-serif (Inter, Poppins)
- Body: High readability (1.6+ line height, generous sizing)
- Emphasis: Semibold for credentials/badges

## Key Components
- **Appointment CTA**: Primary blue, always above fold
- **Doctor Profiles**: Photo, credentials, specialties, patient reviews
- **Appointment Booking**: Simple, friction-free flow
- **Insurance/Hours**: Clear, accessible footer
