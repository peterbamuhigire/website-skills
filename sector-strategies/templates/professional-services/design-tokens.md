# Professional Services: Design Tokens Reference

## Color Philosophy
Authority, expertise, trustworthiness. Sophisticated, not flashy. Premium feel.

**Sector Standard:**
- Primary: Deep navy or charcoal (#1F2937 or #0F172A)
- Accent: One sophisticated color (gold, deep blue, or green)
- Background: Clean white, light gray
- Text: High contrast, professional

## Typography
- Headers: Elegant serif (Lora, Merriweather) or premium sans-serif
- Body: Professional sans-serif
- Display: Minimal use, premium fonts only

## Key Components
- **Service Cards**: Minimal design, emphasis on copy
- **Team Profiles**: Professional photos, credentials, expertise areas
- **Case Study Cards**: Project name, result/impact, learn more CTA
- **Testimonial**: Client name/company, specific quote, impact metrics
