# Professional Services Sector Brief

Copy this template to `docs/sector-brief.md` in your client project and answer each section.

## Brand Identity
- Service type: (Law, consulting, accounting, design, etc.)
- Ideal client: Company size, industry, values?
- Unique positioning vs competitors?

## Visual Identity
- Brand personality: Prestigious? Approachable? Cutting-edge?
- Color palette: (suggest: sophisticated navy/charcoal + one accent)
- Typography: Traditional or modern?

## Trust & Conversion
- Primary CTA: (Schedule consultation, Request proposal, Download case study)
- Key credentials: (Awards, client list, years experience, team expertise)
- Notable case studies or client wins? (3–5)

## Content & Messaging
- Core services: (3–5)
- Team bios and expertise areas?
- Competitor websites for reference? (up to 5)
